Hello Traders

I’m Berwan Barzinji

I've been trading for over three years, and like many others, I used to ignore fundamentals. Not because I didn’t care but because they always felt too complex, scattered, and hard to act on. I relied mostly on charts, indicators, and price action, thinking that was enough.

The more I noticed something all successful traders had in common, they start with the data. The pros don’t trade blindly they look at economic releases, global events, and market sentiment first. Only then do they use technicals to time their entries. That insight changed everything for me.

I'm not a guru or anything special. I’m just someone who loves trading and happens to be an engineer. So I decided to build something I wish I had from day one a tool that breaks down fundamentals in the simplest, clearest way possible. That's why I created fundafun a platform that shows the "why" behind market moves across all assets in the simplest way as possible.

I hope this helps other traders gain confidence, take better trades, and lose less. But building something this fast something that reacts instantly to news from around the world requires resources. That’s why, if you believe in this idea and want to support it, I'm kindly asking for donations. I call it our website because it’s built for all of us.

And finally, I don’t want this to be just a tool I want it to become a community. A healthy, supportive space where traders help each other grow. Join us on Discord or Telegram and be part of something better. Much love to everyone and thank you for being here.
