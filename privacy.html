Privacy Policy

At FundaFun, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy. 1. What Information We Collect We collect only the following information and nothing more: a. Email Address Collected when you voluntarily join our email list for updates, content, or platform-related announcements. b. Optional Donation Information If you choose to make a donation, you can optionally provide the following only if you want it displayed publicly on our "I Donated" section: - Crypto Wallet Address (for recognition purposes only) - Social Media Username (optional) - Name or Alias (optional) - Personal Message (optional) All of the above donation-related fields are voluntary and used only for public display with your consent. 2. How We Use Your Information Email addresses are used solely to send updates, announcements, or content related to the FundaFun platform. Donation information is only published if you fill out the donation display form and explicitly choose to be shown. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. 3. How We Store and Protect Your Data We take your privacy seriously: - All collected data is stored securely and access is limited. - We use encryption and modern security practices to keep your data safe. - We never collect passwords or financial data. 4. Cookies & Tracking We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking. We may use basic cookies only to maintain website functionality (e.g., theme preferences). 5. Third-Party Platforms Donations are processed through third-party crypto platforms. We do not handle or store any payment or transaction data. If you use social media links (e.g., Discord, Telegram), those platforms have their own privacy policies. 6. Your Rights You have full control over your information: - You can unsubscribe from the email list at any time. - You can request that your donation display be updated or removed. - You can request full deletion of any stored personal data. To make any such request, please use our Contact Page. 7. Children’s Privacy This website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make major changes, we will notify users via email (if subscribed) or post a clear notice on the site. 9. Contact If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please reach out via our Contact Page. We are committed to respecting your privacy.
